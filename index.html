Experience Uganda

Uganda is one of Africa’s most exciting tourism destinations. From mountain gorilla trekking to savannah wildlife safaris, visitors experience nature, culture, and adventure in one country.

Top Destinations

Bwindi Impenetrable Forest

Home to endangered mountain gorillas and one of the world's most remarkable wildlife encounters.

Murchison Falls National Park

Experience powerful waterfalls, wildlife safaris, and boat cruises along the Nile River.

Queen Elizabeth National Park

Known for tree-climbing lions, crater lakes, and spectacular wildlife diversity.
