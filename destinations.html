Explore Uganda's Top Destinations

Discover breathtaking landscapes, wildlife, and unforgettable tourism experiences.

Top Tourist Attractions

Bwindi Impenetrable Forest

A UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for mountain gorilla trekking and biodiversity.

  • Mountain Gorilla Trekking
  • Nature Walks
  • Bird Watching

Murchison Falls National Park

Uganda’s largest national park known for wildlife safaris and the powerful Murchison Falls.

  • Game Drives
  • Nile Boat Cruises
  • Bird Watching

Queen Elizabeth National Park

A diverse ecosystem featuring savannah, wetlands, and tree-climbing lions.

  • Tree Climbing Lions
  • Kazinga Channel Boat Safari
  • Chimpanzee Tracking

Lake Victoria

Africa’s largest lake offering beautiful islands, beaches, and fishing tourism.

  • Island Tours
  • Boat Cruises
  • Beach Relaxation

Rwenzori Mountains

Also called the Mountains of the Moon, these peaks offer breathtaking hiking adventures.

  • Mountain Trekking
  • Camping
  • Photography

Jinja – Source of the Nile

East Africa’s adventure capital known for rafting and extreme sports.

  • White Water Rafting
  • Bungee Jumping
  • Kayaking
